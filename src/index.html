Mapping the money behind District 11 candidate Scott Wiener
Outside committees backing District 11 state Senate candidate Scott Wiener have spent more than $1.5 million in the race so far – an amount more than five times larger than what rival candidate Jane Kim has drawn. Most of that money has come from the Equality California Political Action Committee, a gay rights PAC. As of mid-October, it had spent more than any other committee in the race.
The main source of funding for Equality California appears to be an unlikely ally: the California Charter Schools Association, a PAC that has donated more than $400,000. A separate branch of the association has spent close to $380,000 on ads and polling supporting Wiener, and has financially backed an anti-Kim committee that’s mostly funded by venture capitalist Ron Conway.
